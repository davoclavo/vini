Vinidavo

Please use headphones, otherwise your laptop will feedback the microphone.
The request to access camera and microphone appears because we emulate a primitive vine client

Click Add Audio Loop to record a short audio and reveal the next fields

In the end you can type a title for your video and click on Vine it

Adventure mode for laptop users:

After you grant access, but not yet clicked the Add Audio Loop button

Move to a small isolated room

Lower all the volume and increase it step by step until audio feedback starts

Lower the minimum amount of volume to remove the feedback

Create a Shell surface with your hands above the microphone

Reshape the shell and move it around to morph sound
